Banana Pancakes Recipe

Fluffy Banana Pancakes

Recipe by: AllRecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Equipment

  • Mixing bowl
  • Whisk
  • Non-stick skillet or griddle
  • Spatula

Directions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat the egg with milk, then stir in mashed bananas and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just blended.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface; flip and cook until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with maple syrup or toppings of your choice.
